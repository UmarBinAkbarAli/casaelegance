Who We Are

More than design —

Casa Elegance is a Dubai-based full-service interior design studio specialising in residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces. From concept to handover, we manage every detail to deliver spaces that feel both refined and liveable.

Our work spans luxury apartments, premium offices, boutique restaurants, and bespoke fit-outs across the UAE and the wider region. Every project begins with listening, and ends with a space our clients are proud to live and work in.

Our Process

How we bring your vision to life.

01

Discovery

We begin with a detailed brief, site visit, and budget discussion to fully understand your vision, lifestyle, and constraints.

02

Concept Design

Our team develops spatial layouts, mood boards, and material palettes — refined through close collaboration until every detail feels right.

03

Design Development

Detailed technical drawings, furniture schedules, and contractor-ready documentation are prepared, reviewed, and signed off.

04

Delivery

We oversee procurement, site execution, and final snagging — ensuring every element is installed to specification before handover.
