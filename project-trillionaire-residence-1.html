Trillionaire Residence 1

Designed to elevate the short-term rental experience, Trillionaire Residence blends contemporary elegance with hospitality-driven functionality.

ClientAirbnb Host
LocationBusiness Bay, Dubai
ScopeDesign & Build
Completion2025
Project Story

Hospitality-driven design for a premium short-term rental

The design approach focused on creating a bright and sophisticated interior defined by soft neutral tones, refined textures, and timeless material selections.

A standout feature of the residence is the private outdoor jacuzzi terrace, transforming the apartment into a premium lifestyle destination and creating a memorable guest experience.

This project demonstrates how thoughtful design can elevate a residential unit into a distinctive hospitality experience while maximizing its appeal within Dubai's competitive short-term rental market.
