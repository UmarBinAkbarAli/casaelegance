Our Process

From Concept to Completion

A structured approach designed to deliver exceptional results with complete transparency at every stage.

Step 01 1–2 Days

Initial Consultation

We begin by understanding your requirements, lifestyle, budget and project goals through a consultation and site assessment.

Step 02 3–10 Days

Design & Space Planning

Our team develops layouts, mood boards and design concepts that define the vision for your space.

Step 03 2–7 Days

Material & Finish Selection

Carefully curated materials, finishes, fixtures and furnishings are selected to align with the design direction.

Step 04 1–3 Days

Proposal & Project Approval

A detailed quotation, scope of work and project schedule are presented for review and approval.

Step 05 2–10 Weeks

Execution & Project Management

Construction, fitout works, joinery production and installations are coordinated and monitored by our team.

Step 06 Project Complete

Handover & Aftercare

A final quality inspection is completed before handover, ensuring every detail meets our standards.
