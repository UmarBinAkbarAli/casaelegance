Burj115 Premium Office

A high-end workplace designed to balance executive presence, everyday collaboration, and a refined hospitality-driven arrival experience.

Client Private Corporate Office
Location Burj Khalifa District
Scope Design & Build
Completion 2024
Project Story

Crafted for performance, warmth, and prestige

The design direction centered on creating a workplace that feels polished and efficient without becoming cold. Casa Elegance used layered materials, warm lighting, and clean architectural lines to define a space that supports both focused work and client-facing moments.

Every zone was planned around flow: a calm reception sequence, well-balanced meeting rooms, flexible collaboration areas, and executive cabins that offer privacy while staying visually connected to the broader office language.
